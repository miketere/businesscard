  QUICK GUIDE: Get Your PayMongo Webhook Secret
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: GET YOUR NGROK URL
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I've opened http://localhost:4040 in your browser.

On that page, you'll see:
  • Forwarding
    https://xxxxx.ngrok-free.app → http://localhost:3000

COPY the HTTPS URL (the one starting with https://)
Example: https://abc123-def456.ngrok-free.app


STEP 2: CREATE WEBHOOK IN PAYMONGO
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I've opened the PayMongo webhooks page in your browser.

1. Click "Create a new webhook" button

2. In "Endpoint URL" field, paste:
   https://YOUR-NGROK-URL/api/subscriptions/webhook
   
   Replace YOUR-NGROK-URL with the URL from Step 1
   
   Example: https://abc123-def456.ngrok-free.app/api/subscriptions/webhook

3. Scroll down to find "Subscription" section

4. Check these boxes:
   ☑ subscription.activated
   ☑ subscription.updated
   ☑ subscription.past_due
   ☑ subscription.unpaid
   ☑ subscription.invoice.paid
   ☑ subscription.invoice.payment_failed

5. Click "Create webhook" or "Save"


STEP 3: COPY THE WEBHOOK SECRET
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
After creating, PayMongo will show:
  • Webhook Secret: whsec_xxxxxxxxxxxxx

COPY the entire secret (starts with whsec_)


STEP 4: TELL ME THE SECRET
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Just reply with:
  "My webhook secret is: whsec_xxxxxxxxxxxxx"

I'll add it to your .env file automatically!

  TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

If ngrok page doesn't load:
  • Wait 10-15 seconds and refresh
  • Make sure ngrok is running

If you can't find Subscription events:
  • You may need to contact PayMongo support first
  • Look for any event with "subscription" in the name
