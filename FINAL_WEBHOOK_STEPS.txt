✅ Localtunnel is running (no signup needed!)
✅ PayMongo webhooks page should be open

STEP 1: GET YOUR PUBLIC URL
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Look at the PowerShell window that opened.

You'll see:
  your url is: https://xxxxx.loca.lt

COPY that URL (example: https://abc123.loca.lt)


STEP 2: CREATE WEBHOOK IN PAYMONGO
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
In the PayMongo page that opened:

1. Click "Create a new webhook"

2. Endpoint URL field, paste:
   https://YOUR-URL.loca.lt/api/subscriptions/webhook
   
   Replace YOUR-URL with the URL from Step 1
   Example: https://abc123.loca.lt/api/subscriptions/webhook

3. Scroll to "Subscription" section

4. Check these boxes:
   ☑ subscription.activated
   ☑ subscription.updated
   ☑ subscription.past_due
   ☑ subscription.unpaid
   ☑ subscription.invoice.paid
   ☑ subscription.invoice.payment_failed

5. Click "Create webhook"


STEP 3: COPY WEBHOOK SECRET
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
After creating, PayMongo shows:
  Webhook Secret: whsec_xxxxxxxxxxxxx

COPY the entire secret (starts with whsec_)


STEP 4: TELL ME THE SECRET
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Just reply:
  "My webhook secret is: whsec_xxxxxxxxxxxxx"

I'll add it to your .env file right away!
